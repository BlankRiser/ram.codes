## Extras

You can never select a work `that_has_a_underscore` as a seperator, but after adding the below config, you can:

```json title=".vscode/settings.json"
"editor.wordSeparators": "`~!@#$%^&*()-=+[{]}\\|;:'\",.<>/?_"
```
